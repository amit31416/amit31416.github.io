Research & Technical Projects

Temperature Modulated Representation Learning for OSR

Fall '23 - Summer '25

Developed a novel modulation scheme to boost both closed set and open set performance across multiple loss families (cross-entropy, contrastive). Improved closed set accuracy by 1.87% and AUROC by 3.3%.

AI Models for Mammography Screening Recall

Spring '24 - Fall '25

Developed models on two large datasets and analyzed biases across demographic and clinical groups. Implemented a cumulative sum (CUSUM) approach for real-time performance monitoring.

FedNet2Net: Model Growing in Federated Training

Spring '20 - Spring '22

Developed an efficient switching scheme to enlarge model capacity while reducing communication and computational costs at the local client level.

Pruning-based Class Incremental Learning

Spring '22 - Fall '23

Analyzed pruning techniques and utilized GAN/VQVAE-based exemplar generation to efficiently utilize model capacity for new tasks.

GI Disease Detection from Capsule Endoscopy

Apr '16 - May '19

Developed a least square optimization-based saliency extraction scheme using minimal ROI-labeled images and probability density fitting.

Caption Expansion in Vision Language Models

Fall '23

Evaluated CLIP, BLIP, and FLAVA on compositionality benchmarks. Designed new metrics to evaluate synthetic caption quality and alignment.

SOCP Approach for Linear SVMs

Fall '21

Implemented modified SVMs based on Second Order Cone Programming to achieve better balances between error rates and constant training time.

Classical ML for Facial Recognition

Fall '20

Developed Bayesian classifiers, KNN, and SVM algorithms from scratch to classify facial expressions, poses, and subjects.

Epileptic EEG Signal Classification

Fall '18

Applied feature engineering based on local pattern transformations for seizure classification.
